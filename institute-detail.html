Overview

IIT Delhi cutoff 2024 is out for admission to the BTech/ BArch courses. The JoSAA Round 1 Seat Allotment is out in the form of opening and closing ranks for General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS and PWD categories. This year, JoSAA 2024 counselling will consist of 5 Rounds only. Candidates can scroll down to this page to check out the IIT Delhi JEE Advanced cutoff 2024 along with the previous year' cutoff trends. A few more updates are given below:

  • Round 2 seat allotment will be out on Jun 27, 2024 (at 5 PM).
  • UCEED 2024 Round 4 seat allotment for IIT Delhi admission to the B.Des course will be announced on Jun 24, 2024.
  • GATE COAP 2024 Round 7 Seat Allotment for admission to the MTech courses is expected to be released on Jun 21, 2024 (10 AM).
  • IIT Delhi IIT JAM 2024 Round 3 admission list for admission to the MSc course will be out on Jun 21, 2024.

Harsh Khattar
4.5
Reviewed on 10 Jun 2024
I am happy to study in this institute.

Placements: 67% of students in our course were placed this year. The highest package for our course is 45 LPA, and the lowest package is around 10 LPA. The top recruiting companies include Air India, American Express, Hydrobots, Jaguar Land Rover India Limited, Google, and Piramal Finance. Around 40% of students got internships in my course, and they were done at high-tech companies.

Infrastructure: My course provided excellent infrastructure for labs, classrooms, library, Wi-Fi, etc. Everything in our institute is excellent; the food quality is good, and the institute provides a free medical facility 24/7 for students in the IITD hospital. The sports facilities are also excellent here.

Faculty: Our teachers are helpful, well-qualified, and very knowledgeable. The quality of teaching is outstanding. The course curriculum is relevant and prepares students for the industry. The exams are a bit difficult, but if we are regular, they become manageable. The pass percentage is 30%.

Other: I like the courses because they have both core and technical content. So far, everything is good. Events and festivals in our institute are amazing: good crowd, nice connectivity among each other, etc.
